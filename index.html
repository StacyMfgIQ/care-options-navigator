Care Options Navigator

You're not choosing between bad options.
You're choosing the best option for right now.
This tool helps you find it — without pressure, without jargon.

6 care types explained Personalized to your situation 5 minutes Nothing saved or shared

When you're in the middle of a care crisis, the options can feel overwhelming — and every article seems to assume you already know the difference between assisted living and memory care, or in-home care and adult day programs.

This tool asks about your specific situation and recommends the options most likely to fit — with honest explanations of what each one actually involves, what it costs, and what the real tradeoffs are.

One honest note before you begin

There is no perfect answer here. Every family's situation is different. This tool gives you a starting point — not a verdict. Whatever you decide, you are doing it out of love.
